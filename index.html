Announcements.

Welcome to the "Take it easy! English Class"! I'm Aridia Benitez and I will be guiding you on this exciting English language learning adventure. Together we will explore basic concepts in a fun and interactive way, adapting to different levels and ages to make English accessible and stimulating for everyone. In this course, you will develop key communication skills that will enable you to understand, speak and enjoy English in everyday situations. From simple greetings and conversations to appreciating cultural diversity through language, each lesson will bring you closer to mastering this global language. Welcome and let the fun begin by learning English in an easy and relaxed way! So, take it easy and let's learn English!

General Description:

Take it easy! English Course

This course is designed to introduce students to the basic concepts of the English language in an interactive and playful manner. It is aimed at students aged between 10 and 30 years and is adaptable to different levels of English proficiency.

Course Objectives:

  • Understand and use basic English vocabulary and expressions.
  • Engage in simple conversations in English.
  • Develop listening and comprehension skills in English.
  • Familiarize themselves with English pronunciation and intonation.
  • Appreciate cultural diversity through language learning.

Methodology:

Virtual classes through a dedicated course website.

Use of interactive materials and multimedia resources.

Completion of practical tasks.

Scheduled synchronous sessions for group language practice.

Course Duration:

The course will run for 5 weeks, with two 45-minute classes per week, for a total of 10 classes.

Requirements:

  • A device with internet access.
  • Availability to participate in scheduled classes.
  • Interest in active and participatory English language learning.

Teaching Materials:

  • Textbook "Get started" (provided online).
  • Multimedia resources, videos, and online exercises.
  • Tasks and practical activities provided through the course website.

Evaluation Systems:
